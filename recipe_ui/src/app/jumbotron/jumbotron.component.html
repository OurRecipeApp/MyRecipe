About MyRecipeBook

MyRecipeBook is an app that helps you organize your recipes,and create recipes. Using MyRecipeBook's built-in browser, you can save recipes that you have created in the app. MyRecipeBook let's you save your ingredients and access them easily.The delete feature let's you clear the unwanted recipe's. The app let's you upload a picture of your recipe and save it.

KEY FEATURES

Create your own recipe!We understand that you want to invent something of your type in kitchen, create your recipes on our app.


Add a snapshot of your created recipe or you can choose an image from our wide variety of pictures for your recipe's


Search your recipe's with our search function, just with a name of your recipe or anything related to your saved recipe!!


Save your recipe's! All of your recipes are conveniently organized in one central place and always with you!!
